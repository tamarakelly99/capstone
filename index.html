Brandi

Valentina

Ivy Bean

This is Ivy Bean, our newest addition and little baby

She has a "king of the house" personality and was trying to run everything by day one. She is super fearless.

Her (secret) boyfriend is Ander and they usually spend the day playing and spend the night cuddling on their cat tree

Ander

This is Ander, our only boy of the bunch and biggest cuddler.

Since he's a boy cat he's SUPER clingy, when he is not with Ivy, he spends his time following me around the house and begging for constant cuddles.

He's a escape artist and constantly runs outside the first chance he can get. Doesn't happen as often in the winter though when he gets his little paws cold!
